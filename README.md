# Vbee AI Speech Workspace

Nen tang AI cua Vbee giup chuyen audio, video, ghi am va noi realtime thanh transcript, ban dich, subtitle va tai lieu co the quan ly trong mot workspace thong nhat.

## Tai lieu quan trong

- `SETUP.md`: huong dan cai dat/chay backend va frontend.
- `API_INTEGRATION_NOTES.md`: ghi chu tich hop provider speech-to-text.
- `UI_REDESIGN_NOTES.md`: ghi chu cac lan thiet ke lai giao dien.
- `VBEE_BRAND_REPLACE_NOTES.md`: ghi chu thay the nhan dien theo Vbee.

## Chuc nang chinh

- V1: tao user, dang ky, dang nhap va workspace sau dang nhap.
- V2: upload audio/video, ghi am, noi realtime, quan ly transcript.
- Goi cuoc: Free, Standard, Special/Business voi quota tuong ung.
- Checkout: mua goi thong qua order/checkout, cap nhat quota sau khi thanh toan.
- Provider: co the mo rong Vbee, Deepgram, Sonix hoac provider khac qua backend adapter.

## Nguyen tac phat trien

Giao dien va noi dung uu tien nhan dien Vbee: mau tim dam, vang noi bat, logo Vbee, ngon ngu ro rang va luong khach hang de hieu.
